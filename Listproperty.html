Why List with Us?

  • Increased Visibility: Your property will be featured on our user-friendly platform, gaining exposure to a vast audience of potential guests.
  • Easy Booking Management: Our intuitive dashboard allows you to manage bookings, set availability, and update pricing effortlessly.
  • Attract More Guests: Highlight the unique features and amenities of your property, making it stand out and attract more guests.
  • Competitive Pricing: Set your own pricing and take control of your rates to stay competitive in the market.
  • Secure and Trusted: Rest assured that your property is in good hands. We prioritize security and provide a trusted platform for both hosts and guests.

How to Get Started

  1. Create an Account: Sign up with Royal Hotel to create your host account.
  2. Property Details: Provide essential details about your property, including location, pricing, and special offers.
  3. Add Captivating Photos: Showcase the beauty of your property with high-quality images to make a lasting impression.
  4. Connect with Guests: Communicate directly with potential guests, addressing inquiries, and building a rapport.
  5. Receive Bookings: Once your property is listed, start receiving bookings from travelers seeking the perfect accommodation.
